Dr. Hashimi seeks to understand how our social networks form and shape our behaviors. Her research focuses on issues pertaining to policing and policy, peer influence and crime, and violence prevention and intervention efforts. Dr. Hashimi's current projects center on the police role, including how the structure and composition of officer relationships contribute to departmental culture and behavior. She is also involved in efforts to reduce at-risk behaviors, including criminal group and gang involvement, firearm use, and criminal recidivism.
